Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how we earn commissions and how it supports our free tools and content.

Full Transparency

No Extra Cost to You

When you purchase through our affiliate links, you pay exactly the same price as going directly to the retailer.
The commission comes from the retailer's marketing budget.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that contain a tracking code. When you click on these links and make a purchase, the retailer pays us a small commission for referring you.

This is a standard practice used by many websites, blogs, and content creators to monetize their work while providing valuable recommendations to their audience.

Key Point: We only recommend products that we believe are useful for safety professionals and workplaces. Our primary goal is to help you find the right PPE, not just to earn commissions.

Amazon Associates Program

PPE Marts is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases made through our affiliate links to Amazon products.

The Process

  1. Click: You click on an affiliate link on our website
  2. Redirect: You're taken to the retailer's website (Amazon, etc.)
  3. Shop: You browse and purchase products (any product, not just the linked one)
  4. Track: The retailer tracks the sale through our affiliate ID
  5. Commission: We receive a commission for the referral
  6. Support: Commissions help us maintain and improve our free tools

How to Identify Affiliate Links

We clearly identify affiliate links in several ways:

Visual Indicators

  • "Buy Now" buttons: All product purchase buttons are affiliate links
  • Amazon links: Contain "amzn.to" or "amazon.com" with tracking parameters
  • Ad disclosures: "As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases" notices
  • Link styling: Consistent styling for affiliate links throughout the site

URL Structure

Our Amazon affiliate links typically look like:

https://amzn.to/3XxXxXx
https://www.amazon.com/dp/XXXXXXXXXX?tag=ppemarts-20

The "amzn.to" links are shortened versions of our full Amazon affiliate links for cleaner presentation.

Your Privacy & Affiliate Links

Privacy Protection: When you click affiliate links, you're redirected to the retailer's website. We don't track what you purchase or collect any personal information from your purchases.

What Retailers Track

When you click an affiliate link, the retailer may track:

  • That you came from our website (through the affiliate ID)
  • What products you view and purchase
  • Basic demographic information (for their analytics)

This tracking is standard e-commerce practice and is governed by the retailer's privacy policy, not ours.

Cookies and Tracking

Most affiliate programs use cookies to track referrals. These cookies typically:

  • Last 24 hours to 30 days (varies by program)
  • Track purchases within the cookie duration
  • Are first-party cookies from the retailer's domain

You can manage these cookies through your browser settings.
